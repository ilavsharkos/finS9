What is SiSK?

Symposium of students of chemistry and related sciences, which will last for two days this year for the first time.

If you want to present your work to your colleagues, you can do it through an oral or poster presentation.

Register!

If you want to listen to interesting lectures and support your colleagues, you can register as a passive participant.

Register!

The Symposium of Chemistry Students is a scientific event that was held for the first time in 2014, and this is its 9th edition. The aim of the Symposium is to enable chemists to connect and learn from each other. If that sounds tempting to you, apply and join us at the Department of Chemistry of Faculty of Science in Zagreb on the 11th and 12th of October 2024!


You don't have to worry about registration fees because it's completely free!
